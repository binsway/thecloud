Comfortable Accommodation

Tea Elanza in Munnar offers family rooms with private bathrooms, balconies, and stunning mountain views. Each room is well-furnished and includes a tea and coffee maker, TV, free toiletries, and a seating area. Guests can enjoy the peaceful ambiance and scenic beauty right from their rooms.

Essential Facilities

Guests enjoy free WiFi throughout the property, ensuring seamless connectivity. The hotel also provides a terrace, a spacious dining area, and free on-site private parking for guests' convenience. Additional amenities include a sofa bed, 24-hour front desk service, daily housekeeping, and laundry services.

Dining Options

Tea Elanza offers an on-site restaurant serving delicious local and continental cuisine. Guests can start their day with a complimentary breakfast and enjoy freshly brewed tea from the nearby plantations. Room service is also available for added comfort.

Convenient Location

Located 93 km from Cochin International Airport, Tea Elanza is in close proximity to popular tourist attractions. The Munnar Tea Museum is just 13 km away, while Mattupetty Dam (22 km) and Anamudi Peak (27 km) offer breathtaking experiences. Adventure seekers can explore nearby trekking trails and nature walks.
